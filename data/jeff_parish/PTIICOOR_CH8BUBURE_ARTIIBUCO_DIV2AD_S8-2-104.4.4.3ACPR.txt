All third party providers shall make known to the appropriate regulatory section of the department their intent to perform inspections; coordinate their inspection timing with the appropriate regulatory section of the department; the third party shall document his inspection results on a form provided for such purposes by the director; and all third party providers are hereby bound to follow the inspection prerequisites stated in section 8-2-104.10.5 of this chapter before performing any inspections.
All third party providers shall present their valid registration when submitting inspection or plan reviews to the parish.
The director of the department may at anytime invalidate a third party provider registration if said third party violates the procedures herein regulating their interaction with the parish.
