The code official may revoke a permit upon determination the construction, erection, alteration, repair, moving, demolition, installation or (re) placement of the building, structure, electrical, gas, mechanical or plumbing systems for which the permit was issued is in violation of, or not in conformity with the provisions of this chapter or other codes enforced by the code official.
