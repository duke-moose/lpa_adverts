In interpreting the provisions of this chapter, these shall be held to be the minimum requirements for the promotion of the public safety, health, convenience, comfort, morals, prosperity and general welfare. Where this chapter imposes a greater restriction or higher standard than is imposed or required by any other provision or regulation of this code, the provisions of this chapter shall govern.
(Ord. No. 20783, § 3(XXVI), 9-22-99; Ord. No. 25405 , § 95, 8-9-17)
