The code official shall submit a report to the governing authority annually covering the work of the department for the preceding year. The code official may incorporate in said report a summary of the decisions of the board of standards and appeals during said year. The code official may also incorporate in said report a summary of any recommendations for amendments to this chapter, its fees or related law(s).
