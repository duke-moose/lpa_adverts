Sec. 2-161.1. - Qualifications, appointment and salary of director.

(a)
Qualifications. [The director of budgeting shall be qualified by education, training and prior administrative and/or management experience.]
(b)
Appointment. The director of budgeting shall be appointed by the parish president with the approval of council.
(c)
Salary. The salary of the director of budgeting shall be established by the parish president within the pay grade for the position as defined in the executive pay plan.
(Ord. No. 18471, § 3, 2-19-92; Ord. No. 25736 , § 5, 2-6-19)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 25736 , § 5, adopted Feb. 6, 2019, amended § 2-161.1 and in so doing changed the title from "Qualifications and appointment of director" to "Qualifications, appointment and salary of director," as set out herein.
Sec. 2-161.2. - Duties of the director.

The director of budgeting shall be responsible for:
(1)
The development, preparation, administration and monitoring of the parish operating budget;
(2)
The preparation and continual update of a budget manual;
(3)
Ensuring that all departments operate within the constraints of the current adopted operating budget;
(5)
The preparation, analyses and interpretation of budgetary data for outside parties as requested.
(Ord. No. 18471, § 3, 2-19-92)
