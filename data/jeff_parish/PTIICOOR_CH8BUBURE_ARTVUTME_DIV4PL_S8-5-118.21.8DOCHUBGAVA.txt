Sec. 8-5-118.21.8. - Double check-double gate valves.

The chief plumbing inspector or his designee may authorize installation of approved, double check-double gate valve assemblies with test cocks as protective devices against backflow in connections between a potable water system, and other fluid systems which present no significant health hazard in the judgment of the chief plumbing inspector or his designee.
