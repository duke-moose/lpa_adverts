Sec. 8-2-105.3. - Quorum and voting.

A simple majority of the board shall constitute a quorum. In the absence of a quorum the board shall take no vote. In varying any provision of this chapter the affirmative votes of the majority present, but not less than three (3) affirmative votes, shall be required. In modifying a decision of the code official, not less than four (4) affirmative votes shall be required.
Board members shall not act in any case in which they have a personal or financial interest.
