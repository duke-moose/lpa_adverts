Sec. 8-2-101.3.7. - Maintenance.

All buildings, structures, electrical, gas, mechanical and plumbing systems, both existing and new, and all parts thereof, shall be maintained in a safe and sanitary, code-complying condition. All devices, systems or safeguards, which are required by the technical codes when constructed, altered or repaired, shall be maintained in good working order. The owner, or owner's designated agent, shall be responsible for the maintenance of buildings, structures, electrical, gas, mechanical and plumbing systems.
