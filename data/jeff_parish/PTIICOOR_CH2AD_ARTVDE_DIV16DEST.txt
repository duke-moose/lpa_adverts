Sec. 2-347. - Qualifications, appointment and salary of director.

(a)
Qualifications. The director of streets shall be qualified by education and at least five (5) years of related management experience and devote his entire efforts to the purpose for which this department/principal office has been created and perform any other such activities and/or responsibilities as the parish president may direct.
(b)
Appointment. The director of streets shall be appointed by the parish president with the approval of the council.
(c)
Salary. The salary of the director of streets shall be established by the parish president within the pay grade for the position as defined in the executive pay plan.
(Code 1961, § 2-482; Ord. No. 25736 , § 24, 2-6-19)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 25736 , § 24, adopted Feb. 6, 2019, amended § 2-347 and in so doing changed the title from "Qualifications and appointment of director" to "Qualifications, appointment and salary of director," as set out herein.

The director of streets shall assist the public works director in directing, controlling and supervising the following functions:
(1)
Maintenance of the parish-wide street network;
(2)
Directing the operations of the park and parkway division;
(3)
Maintenance of parish rights-of-way and enforcement of the weed and pollution control ordinance;
(4)
Providing canal maintenance support for the drainage department as required.
(Code 1961, § 2-481)
