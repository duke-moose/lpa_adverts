The provisions of the following sections shall constitute, be known, and cited as "The Building and Related Construction Codes of Jefferson Parish" hereinafter referred to as "this Article". These provisions replace Ordinance No. 20434, known as "The Building Code of Jefferson Parish, Louisiana", adopted on August 19, 1998. To the extent any existing ordinances are contrary to these provisions, they are repealed in that respect only.
