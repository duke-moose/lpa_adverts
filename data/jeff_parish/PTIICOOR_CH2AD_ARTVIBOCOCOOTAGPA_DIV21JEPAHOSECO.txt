The Jefferson Parish Homeland Security Committee shall be composed of nineteen (19) members, as follows:
(1)
Each councilperson shall appoint one member by resolution;
(2)
The parish president, or his designee;
(3)
The Sheriff of Jefferson Parish, or his designee;
(4)
The chiefs of police of the municipalities of Kenner, Harahan, Gretna, Westwego, Jean Lafitte and Grand Isle, or their designees;
(5)
The Superintendent of the Jefferson Parish Public School System, or his designee,
(6)
A representative of the Jefferson Parish Fire Department's Presidents and Chiefs Association;
(7)
A representative from Jefferson Parish Emergency Medical Services; and
(8)
A mayor of one (1) of the municipalities of Kenner, Gretna, Westwego, Harahan, Lafitte, or Grand Isle, with said mayoral members to serve one-year memberships on the committee in accordance with the following rotation:
1st year: Mayor of Grand Isle;
2nd year: Mayor of Gretna;
3rd year: Mayor of Harahan;
4th year: Mayor of Kenner;
5th year: Mayor of Lafitte;
6th year: Mayor of Westwego.
The rotation of mayors serving on the committee shall continue in the rotation cycle established upon the adoption of Resolution No. 106524 on October 18, 2006. In the event that a mayor declines membership on the committee, the rotation will pass to the next mayor whose term on the Jefferson Parish Homeland Security Committee occurs completely within his term as mayor. In such case, the rotation will continue until all mayors have served their one-year term or declined such membership at which time a new rotation will be established.
(Ord. No. 23956, § 1, 2-2-11; Ord. No. 25220 , § 1, 9-21-16; Ord. No. 25979 , § 1, 4-8-20)
Sec. 2-798. - Functions and duties.

(a)
The Jefferson Parish Homeland Security Committee shall meet as needed to formulate policy and take necessary actions to ensure that Jefferson Parish shall receive an equitable portion of the funds allocated by the federal government to the New Orleans metropolitan area to enhance homeland security measures in said area; and to establish priorities for the expenditure of said funds for the enhancement of homeland security within Jefferson Parish.
(b)
The committee shall make recommendations to the Jefferson Parish Council regarding the expenditures of Federal Homeland Security grants awarded to Jefferson Parish.
(Ord. No. 23956, § 1, 2-2-11)
