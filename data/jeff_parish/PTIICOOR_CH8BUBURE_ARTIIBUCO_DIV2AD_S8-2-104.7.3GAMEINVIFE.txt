Sec. 8-2-104.7.3. - Gas and mechanical investigation/violation fees.

Whenever alleged any gas or mechanical work, for which a filing is required under the provisions of this chapter, has commenced without a proper application for inspection (filing), an investigation shall be made. The code official may impose a violation fee for any non-permitted or non-filed work infractions. The violation fee shall be three hundred dollars ($300.00). This violation fee is in addition to the original required permit or filing fees or other penalties imposed by this chapter.
