A potable water system shall be protected against backflow and backsiphoning by providing and maintaining at each outlet:
1.
Air gaps: An air gap as specified in section 13.3.8.2 is known to be between the potable water outlet and the flood level rim of the fixture it supplies or between the outlet and any other source of contamination.
2.
Backflow preventer: An approved backflow preventer device or vacuum breaker to prevent the drawing of contamination into the potable water system.
