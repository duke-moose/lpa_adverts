The Jefferson Parish Charter Advisory Board is hereby created pursuant to Section 4.06 of the Jefferson Parish Charter which empowers the council to create by ordinance such advisory boards as it deems necessary. This board shall not be construed to be a charter commission within the meaning of Louisiana Revised Statutes 33:1395, et seq.
(Ord. No. 18600, § 1, 10-7-92)
Sec. 2-875.30. - Composition; terms, vacancies.

(a)
The Jefferson Parish Charter Advisory Board shall be composed of fifteen (15) members, all of whom shall be registered voters of Jefferson Parish, as follows:
(1)
Seven (7) members, one (1) appointed by each council member;
(2)
One (1) member appointed by the Westbank Business Industrial Association;
(3)
Two (2) members appointed by the parish president;
(4)
One (1) member appointed by the Jefferson Chamber of Commerce;
(5)
One (1) member appointed by the Jefferson Parish Economic Development Commission (JEDCO);
(6)
One (1) member appointed by the Jefferson Business Council;
(7)
One (1) member appointed by the Nu Alpha Lambda Chapter of the Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity; and
(8)
One (1) member appointed by the Delgado Community College Westbank Campus.
(b)
All appointments to the Jefferson Parish Charter Advisory Board to be made by the Council or by members of the council in accordance with this section shall be by council resolution. Each other appointment to the Jefferson Parish Charter Advisory Board shall become effective when written notification of said appointment is received by the parish clerk from the person authorized to make such appointment in accordance with this section.
(c)
The members of the Jefferson Parish Charter Advisory Board shall serve for one (1) year concurrent terms beginning with the first meeting of the board.
(d)
Vacancies on the board shall be filled for the unexpired term of the vacant member by appointment in the manner provided for in this section by the person who appointed the vacant member. A vacancy shall be deemed to exist if a member misses three (3) consecutive meetings of the board.
(Ord. No. 18600, § 1, 10-7-92; Ord. No. 18749, § 1, 5-12-93; Ord. No. 24016, § 1, 5-25-11; Ord. No. 25978 , § 1, 4-8-20)

(a)
The Jefferson Parish Charter Advisory Board shall study and review the existing Jefferson Parish Charter and shall submit recommendations to the Jefferson Parish council for any revisions or changes which, in the opinion of the board, should be made to the Jefferson Parish Charter. These recommendations shall be approved by a majority of the members of the board and shall be submitted to the council no later than one (1) year from the date of the first meeting of the board.
(b)
The Jefferson Parish Charter Advisory Board shall establish its own by-laws and shall elect its own officers.
(Ord. No. 18600, § 1, 10-7-92)
