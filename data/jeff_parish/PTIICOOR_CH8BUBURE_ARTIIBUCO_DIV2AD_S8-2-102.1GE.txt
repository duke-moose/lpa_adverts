The code official is hereby authorized and directed to enforce the provisions of this chapter. For the purposes of enforcing the provisions of this chapter the code official and designated deputies are law enforcement officers. Such law enforcement officers shall be sworn-in and appropriately commissioned. These deputies shall also be provided with badges of authority and photographic identification indicating such authority.
The code official is further authorized to render interpretations of this chapter, which are consistent with its spirit and purpose.
