There is hereby created a public works change order review committee for the purpose of providing input to the parish council on certain change orders as specified in section 2-590 herein proposed for publicly-bid contracts between Jefferson Parish and any other person, firm or business; and on any matters related to public contracts on which the Jefferson Parish Council may from time to time request input from the committee.
(Ord. No. 24291, § 2, 6-13-12)
Sec. 2-587. - Composition; terms.

(a)
The public works change order review committee shall be composed of six (6) members.
(b)
There shall be one (1) duly authorized representative from each of the following departments: Public works, research and budget analysis, finance, parish attorney. The parish attorney's representative shall be a non-voting member.
(c)
There shall be two (2) appointments made by the parish council, one (1) of which shall be designated by the parish council as the chair of the committee.
(d)
No member of the public works change order review committee shall perform any services or receive any compensation under a Jefferson Parish bid contract, nor shall any person, firm or business for whom the committee member works or by whom the committee member is employed perform any services or receive any compensation under a Jefferson Parish bid contract during the member's term on the committee. In addition, no member of the public works change order review committee shall submit a bid nor shall any person, firm or business for whom the committee member works or by whom the committee member is employed submit a bid in response to a Jefferson Parish bid proposal during the member's term on the committee
(e)
The term of each member of the committee shall be for two (2) years, except that a committee member appointed by the council may be removed by the council at any time during the committee member's term without cause. Each committee member shall continue to serve beyond the term of his appointment until his successor is appointed.
(Ord. No. 24291, § 2, 6-13-12)

Sec. 2-589. - Meetings.

(a)
The committee shall meet on the Monday prior to every council meeting which has a change order of the type specified in section 2-590 herein on the agenda for the council meeting.
(b)
A representative from the department requesting the change order and if the director of the requesting department deems necessary, a representative from any contracted design professional or project manager recommending the change order shall present to the committee all information necessary for the committee to make an informed recommendation.
(c)
The committee shall make a recommendation in writing to the council that the council stating whether, in the committee's opinion, the council should either approve or deny each change order.
(d)
The committee's recommendation shall be transmitted to the council prior to the council meeting which has the change order which is the subject of the recommendation on its agenda.
(e)
The committee shall also provide to the council written reasons for each recommendation of denial that the committee makes.
(Ord. No. 24291, § 2, 6-13-12)

The public works change order review committee shall review all public works-related change orders which increase or decrease the contract amount by either twenty-five (25) percent or more of the original contract amount or by one hundred fifty thousand dollars ($150,000.00) or more; or which, when added to all other change orders previously approved for the contract, cumulatively increase or decrease the contract amount by either twenty-five (25) percent or more of the original contract amount or by one hundred fifty thousand dollars ($150,000.00) or more.
(Ord. No. 24291, § 2, 6-13-12)

Each member of the public works change order committee, other than the parish attorney's designee, shall have one (1) vote per change order under review with regard to the recommendation that the committee makes to the council with regard to the change order.
(Ord. No. 24291, § 2, 6-13-12)

(a)
All members of the committee shall serve without compensation.
(b)
Any member of the committee who is not employee of the parish nor any person(s) or firm(s) for which said member is working, are prohibited from doing work for Jefferson Parish as either a prime contractor or subcontractor and from doing work for any person or firm appointed to do work for Jefferson Parish during the duration of the member's term and for six (6) months thereafter.
(c)
Members of the public works change order review committee shall be subject to all rules and regulations governing ethics for public officials in Louisiana during their tenure on the committee.
(Ord. No. 24291, § 2, 6-13-12)
