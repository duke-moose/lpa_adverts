On each floor of garages where not more than three (3) motor vehicles are serviced and stored, interceptors shall have a minimum capacity of six (6) cubic feet and one (1) cubic foot capacity shall be added for each vehicle up to ten (10) vehicles. Above ten (10) vehicles, the chief plumbing inspector or his designee shall determine the size of the interceptor required.
