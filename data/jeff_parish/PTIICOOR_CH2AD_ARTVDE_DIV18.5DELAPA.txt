The Department of Lafreniere Park and the position of Director of Lafreniere Park are hereby created to assist the department of parks and recreation, which this department and position shall have direct administrative supervision over Lafreniere Park.
(Ord. No. 25861 , § 2, 9-18-19)
Sec. 2-380. - Qualifications, appointment and salary of director.

(a)
Qualifications. The Director of Lafreniere Park shall be qualified by:
(1)
Associates Degree in Business Management, Park Management or closely related field supplemented by five (5) years current experience and/or training that includes direct management responsibility, including managing large events, as well as park maintenance, development and planning responsibility; or
(2)
An equivalent combination of education, training, and experience.
(b)
Appointment. The Director of Lafreniere Park shall be appointed by the Parish President with the approval of the council.
(c)
Salary. The salary of the director of parks and recreation shall be established by the parish president within the pay grade for the position as defined in the executive pay plan.
(Ord. No. 25861 , § 3, 9-18-19)
Sec. 2-381. - Duties of the director—Generally.

The Director of Lafreniere Park shall:
(1)
Manage operations of Lafreniere Park and formulate and evaluate operating policies, programs and procedures relative to maintaining the park, and act as department liaison to Patrons of Lafreniere Park.
(2)
Directs department budget development; administers department budget; approves expenditures.
(3)
Oversees special events and projects to achieve goals for the park, and performs oversight and/or coordination of marketing and fundraising conducted on behalf of, or for the benefit of, Lafreniere Park.
(4)
Promotes, implements and monitors landscaping maintenance of grounds and facilities including landscape crew operations.
(5)
Responsible for social media management for Lafreniere Park which includes overseeing the regular download, upload, and/or entering of content used for social media including but not limited to departmental web page, Twitter, Facebook and Instagram.
(6)
Prepare reports on departmental operations evaluating performance against established objectives, and prepares special reports on operating problems or plans, as required.
(7)
Attends and participates in council and other meetings; advises council, parish president and administration regarding Lafreniere Park issues.
(8)
Responds to public's questions or concerns, may be required to speak to local groups or clubs on issues related to Lafreniere Park.
(9)
Responds to emergency situations.
(Ord. No. 25861 , § 4, 9-18-19)
