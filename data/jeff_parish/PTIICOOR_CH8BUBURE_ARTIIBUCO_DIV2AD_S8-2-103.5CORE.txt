Sec. 8-2-103.5. - Contractor responsibilities.

It shall be the duty of every contractor who shall make contracts for the installation or repairs of building, structure, electrical, gas, mechanical or plumbing systems, for which a permit or application is required, to comply with state or local rules and regulations concerning licensing and registration which the State of Louisiana and the Parish of Jefferson have adopted. Upon demand of the code official, all contractors shall produce a certificate of good standing from the Jefferson Parish Sheriff's Bureau of Revenue and Taxation Division before any Jefferson Parish contractor's license/certificate shall be issued or renewed.
All contractors required to be licensed by the state shall produce a certificate of resident/non-resident status from the Louisiana Department of Revenue and Taxation.
