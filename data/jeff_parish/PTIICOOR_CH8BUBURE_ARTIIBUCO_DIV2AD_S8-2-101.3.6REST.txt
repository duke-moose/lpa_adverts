Standards and other Codes cited in the referenced codes shall be considered an integral part of this chapter without separate adoption. If specific portions of a standard are denoted by code text, only those portions of the standard shall be enforced. In the event Code provisions conflict with a standard, the Code provisions shall be enforced. Permissive and advisory provisions in a standard shall not be construed as mandatory.
