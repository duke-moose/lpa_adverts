The department of central garage and the position of director of central garage is hereby created, which department and position shall have direct administrative supervision over repair and maintenance of all parish-owned vehicles and heavy equipment.
(Code 1961, § 2-470)
Sec. 2-332. - Qualifications, appointment and salary of director.

(a)
Qualifications. The director of central garage shall be qualified by professional training and at least four (4) years' relevant management experience for this position and devote his entire efforts to this purpose for which this department/ principal office has been created and any other such activities and/or responsibilities as the parish president may direct.
(b)
Appointment. The director of central garage shall be appointed by the parish president with the approval of the council.
(c)
Salary. The salary of the director of central garage shall be established by the parish president within the pay grade for the position as defined in the executive pay plan.
(Code 1961, § 2-472; Ord. No. 25736 , § 23, 2-6-19)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 25736 , § 23, adopted Feb. 6, 2019, amended § 2-332 and in so doing changed the title from "Qualifications and appointment of director" to "Qualifications, appointment and salary of director," as set out herein.
Sec. 2-333. - Duties of director.

The director of central garage shall:
(1)
Manage department operations of central garage, including establishment of a comprehensive preventative maintenance program for parish vehicles and repair and maintenance of parish vehicles;
(2)
Provide policy guidance to the president in the area of the central garage;
(3)
Direct administrative and field staff assigned;
(4)
Formulate and evaluate operating policies, programs and procedures relating to central garage;
(5)
Prepare reports on departmental operations evaluating performance against established objectives, and special reports on operating problems or plans as required;
(6)
Coordinate budget formulation and management activities in the department of central garage;
(7)
Centralize control and standardization of specifications and purchase of vehicles and operating equipment, with cooperation of all directors, to provide greater standardization and improved efficiency/usage.
(Code 1961, § 2-471; Ord. No. 24677, § 1, 2-5-14)
