Sec. 8-2-101.4. - Department of inspection and code enforcement.

The department of inspection and code enforcement (and the director thereof), as constituted by the Jefferson Parish Code of Ordinances, Chapter 2, Division 6, section 2-196, shall be responsible for administration and enforcement of the provisions of this chapter. The person charged with the management of the department of inspection and code enforcement, hereinafter referred to as "the department"; shall be the director, hereinafter also referred to as the "code official."
