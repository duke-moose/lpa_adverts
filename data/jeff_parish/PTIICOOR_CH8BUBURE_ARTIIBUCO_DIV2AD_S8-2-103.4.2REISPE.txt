If, in the opinion of the code official, the application for a permit and accompanying contract documents do not substantially conform to the requirements of the technical codes, zoning ordinance, or other pertinent laws the code official shall not issue a permit. The code official shall return the contract documents and inform the applicant of the permit refusal. The code official's refusal shall, when requested, be in writing and shall contain the reason for refusal. All plan processing fees are non-refundable and shall not be returned to the applicant.
