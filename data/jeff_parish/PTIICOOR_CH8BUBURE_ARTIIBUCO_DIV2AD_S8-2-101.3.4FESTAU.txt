The provisions of this chapter shall not be held to deprive any federal or state agency, or any applicable governing authority having jurisdiction, of any power or authority which it had on the effective date of the adoption of this chapter or of any remedy then existing for the enforcement of its orders. The provisions of this chapter shall qualify and supplement any and all laws of the State of Louisiana relative to buildings or structures.
The provisions of this chapter shall not deprive any individual or corporation of its legal rights under law.
