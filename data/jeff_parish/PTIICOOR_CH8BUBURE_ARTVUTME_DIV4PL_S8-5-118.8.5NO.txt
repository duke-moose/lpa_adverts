The office of the chief plumbing inspector shall be notified when the work is ready for inspection before the close of business on the day previous to the one on which the inspection is desired. All work shall be left uncovered until inspected and approved. If any plumbing system or part thereof, which is installed, altered, or repaired, is covered before being inspected tested and approved, as prescribed in this Code, it shall be uncovered for inspection after notice to uncover the work has been issued to the responsible party by the chief plumbing inspector. Notices of violations shall be written and delivered, or mailed to the person responsible.
