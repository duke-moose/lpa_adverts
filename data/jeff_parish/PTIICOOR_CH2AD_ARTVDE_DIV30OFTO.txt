The office of tourism and the position of coordinator of the office of tourism are hereby created.
(1)
Position created; purpose. The position of coordinator of the office of tourism is hereby created to provide Jefferson Parish with essential tourism leadership and management during the office creation, start-up, developmental and planning phase, and professional leadership and administrative management upon establishing the office of tourism as a vital functioning, productive and public service, economic-enhancing component of the Jefferson Parish community, and to develop long term tourism plans of action, convention planning and assistance, to provide fiscal accountability and integrity for the daily operations of the office of tourism, and to represent Jefferson Parish at the highest possible level in its efforts to communicate and promote the maximum Jefferson Parish tourism experience.
(2)
Essential functions. The coordinator of tourism under the parish president shall:
a.
Plan, direct, and coordinator Jefferson Parish's active participation in tourism and to ensure that Jefferson Parish's interests in promoting tourism are maximized;
b.
Plan, coordinate, direct, originate, and present tourism development activities, programs, and initiatives to stimulate local, regional, state, national, and international awareness and interest in Jefferson Parish and its wealth of diverse cultural, social, recreational, family, and interesting destinations;
c.
Plan, coordinate and present informational materials and programs to stimulate interest and attract visitors to Jefferson Parish destinations, including convention planning and assistance;
d.
Directly supervise and administer all aspects of operations of the office of tourism;
e.
Serve as tourism liaison for Jefferson Parish Government and Parish business and civic groups, metro tourism organizations, State of Louisiana, and others active in the tourism industry;
f.
Represent the parish and the parish president for issues relating to tourism and serve as technical advisor to the parish for all tourism related matters.
(3)
Qualifications. The coordinator of the office of tourism shall be qualified by education, training, and prior successful work experience to direct and coordinate all aspects of the office of tourism and shall devote his/her entire efforts to the purpose, goals, and objectives for which this office and position has been created.
(4)
Appointment. The position of coordinator of tourism shall be appointed by the parish president with the approval of the council.
(5)
Salary. The salary for the position of coordinator of tourism shall be established by the parish president within Group III of the executive pay plan.
