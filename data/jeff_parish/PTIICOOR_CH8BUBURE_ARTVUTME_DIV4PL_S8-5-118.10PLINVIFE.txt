Sec. 8-5-118.10. - Plumbing investigation/violation fees.

Whenever alleged work, for which a plumbing filing is required, under the provisions of this Code, has commenced without a proper authorization of same, an investigation fee shall be paid. The code official may impose an investigation/violation fee for such non-permitted work infractions. The investigation/violation fee shall be a maximum of three hundred dollars ($300.00) in addition to the regular filing fees imposed by this Code.
