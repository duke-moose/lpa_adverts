(a)
Qualifications. The director of parkways shall be qualified by education, training and at least five (5) years of administrative and/or management experience and shall devote his entire effort to this purpose for which this department has been created.
(b)
Appointment. The director of parkways shall be appointed by the parish president with the approval of the council.
(c)
Salary. The salary of the director of parkways shall be established by the parish president within the pay grade for the position as defined in the executive pay plan.
(Ord. No. 17550, § 2, 7-13-88; Ord. No. 23957, § 7, 2-2-11; Ord. No. 25736 , § 25, 2-6-19)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 25736 , § 25, adopted Feb. 6, 2019, amended § 2-350 and in so doing changed the title from "Qualifications and appointment of director" to "Qualifications, appointment and salary of director," as set out herein.
Sec. 2-351. - Duties of director.

The director of parkways shall:
(1)
Manage department operations in parkways by coordinating budget formulation and management activities with the advice and consent of the director of public works;
(2)
Prepare reports for the parish president and the director of public works on departmental operations evaluating performance against established objectives; on special problems; and on plans as required for review;
(3)
Direct administrative and field staff assigned to this department;
(4)
Formulate and evaluate operating policies, programs and procedures relating to beautification projects on public rights-of-way with the advice and consent of the director of public works;
(5)
Supervise maintenance operation on medians and other public property including street sweeping, grass cutting, gardening, litter removal and sign removals;
(6)
Set policy for the trim and removal of trees/stumps on parish rights-of-way.
(Ord. No. 17550, § 3, 7-13-88; Ord. No. 23957, § 8, 2-2-11)
