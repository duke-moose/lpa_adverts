Sec. 2-960. - Proposals for personal service; contracts; funding.

The Jefferson Parish Council hereby authorizes the department director to accept proposals from persons or firms interested in providing personal services to the parish. The director or his designee shall execute a contract with the persons or firms, selected to provide personal services under this division. No personal services by the persons or firms selected shall be authorized and no payment shall be made to the persons or firms selected until it is shown that the current annual budget contains sufficient monies as determined by the finance director.
(Ord. No. 19957, § 1, 2-26-97)
