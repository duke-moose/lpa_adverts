Sec. 8-2-102.2.3. - Emergency.

When a clear and immediate danger to the health, life or safety of any person(s) exists and the public's health, safety or welfare is thereby threatened, the code official may, without prior notice to the owner, enter onto any property in order to abate an imminent dangerous condition(s). After the emergency has passed the code official shall notify the owner of the action(s) taken.
