Sec. 8-2-104.10.3. - Inspections prior to certificate of occupancy or completion.

The code official shall inspect or cause to be inspected periodically all construction or work for which a building permit is issued or filing has been accepted. The code official shall make a record of every such inspection and cite any apparent violations of the technical codes.
A final inspection shall be made of every building, structure, or service system(s) upon completion, and prior to the issuance of the certificate of completion.
