The animal shelter advisory board shall be composed of nine (9) members. Each councilperson shall appoint one (1) member, the parish president shall appoint two (2) members, and the appointments shall receive concurrence of the parish council by resolution of the council. All members of the board shall be considered electors of the parish, who hold no other public office or position in the parish, other than a committee constituted pursuant to this division. The board shall elect its own chairperson, who shall serve one (1) year or until he is re-elected or his successor is elected. The parish attorney or designee shall be the advisor for the board.
(Code 1961, § 4-12(b); Ord. No. 24103, § 1, 8-31-11)

The animal shelter advisory board is an advisory board and shall have the authority to:
(a)
Investigate and present to the animal shelter director all available sources of parish, state and federal funds available to the shelter to support various programs and projects. The board may participate in assisting the shelter to obtain such funds.
(b)
Upon request from the parish president, parish council or animal shelter director, the board shall conduct appropriate research to formulate recommendations regarding operations of the shelter as identified by the council or director. The board shall provide support documentation to the requesting authority for all recommendations made.
(c)
Formulate recommendations, including amendments to ordinances, along with supporting documentation to the animal shelter director.
(Code 1961, § 4-12(c); Ord. No. 20257, § 1, 1-14-98; Ord. No. 20681, § 1, 5-5-99; Ord. No. 24103, § 2 8-31-11)
