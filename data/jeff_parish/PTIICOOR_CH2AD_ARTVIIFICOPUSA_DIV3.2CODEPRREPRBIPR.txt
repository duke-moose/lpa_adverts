(a)
This list is intended to provide the parish with the names of contractors, businesses or vendors that may be interested in competing for residential housing or commercial rehabilitation contracts and/or purchase orders.
(b)
Advertisements for the establishment and maintenance of bidders' lists by the Jefferson Parish Department of Community Development is hereby authorized by the adoption of this section. From the advertisements and submission of required documents, a list of pre-qualified bidders shall be compiled by the department of community development and supplied to the purchasing department for solicitation of rehabilitation projects. Those prequalified bidders on the list will be mailed notices inviting bids for projects involving work for the rehabilitation. The list is for convenience only and does not represent a recommendation, an endorsement, or an indication of responsibility in respect to its ability to successfully perform and complete rehabilitation activities for the parish.
(c)
To be added to the list of pre-qualified bidders, those interested bidders shall supply the following:
(1)
Parish of Jefferson Contractor Registration List Application;
(2)
Valid contractor license issued by the State of Louisiana, if applicable;
(3)
Submit proof of ability to obtain the minimum insurance requirements in accordance with the policies and procedures of the Jefferson Parish Residential Housing and Commercial Rehabilitation Program;
(4)
Current occupational license;
(5)
Submit proof of ability to secure a surety bond, standby letter of credit or provide cash collateral, if applicable;
(6)
Able to operate as a contractor in Jefferson Parish, if applicable;
(7)
Completed affirmation of registration to do business with the U.S. government (verifying non-debarment).
(d)
Bidders are allowed to apply to be added to the prequalified bidders list in accordance policies and procedures of the Jefferson Parish Residential Housing and Commercial Rehabilitation Program. The entire list of prequalified bidders will be subject to requalification not less than every two (2) years, unless removed in accordance with the policies and procedures of the Jefferson Parish Residential Housing and Commercial Rehabilitation Program.
(Ord. No. 25517 , § 1, 2-21-18)

(a)
Bidding process. The selection of a successful bidder in the Residential Housing or Commercial Rehabilitation Program will follow the procurement process, with bidding, awards and contract execution coordinated by the Purchasing Department in accordance with the policies and procedures of the Jefferson Parish Residential Housing and Commercial Rehabilitation Program.
(b)
Insurance for bidders. To execute a contract in connection with the Parish of Jefferson's Residential Housing and Commercial Rehabilitation Program, a bidder must submit insurance stating that such bidder carries the types and amounts of coverage required for this program, as stated in the respective program policies and procedures for the Jefferson Parish Residential Housing or Commercial Rehabilitation Program.
(Ord. No. 25517 , § 1, 2-21-18)
