Sec. 2-611. - Created; composition.

The technical evaluation committee is hereby established for the purpose of evaluating proposals for architectural, engineering, surveying, and laboratory and field testing services for parish projects and shall be composed of:
(1)
The director of the department directly involved with each project under consideration requiring professional services. In the event the director was selected by the parish president to serve a one (1) year term as a representative of the administration on the technical evaluation committee, the director of the engineering department shall represent that department in his place. In the event that more than one (1) department is directly involved with the project under consideration, the parish president shall select one (1) director to serve on the committee from among all the directors of the departments involved. For hospital service district projects, the president and CEO of the hospital involved or his designee shall replace the department director on the committee;
(2)
A Louisiana registered professional engineer in the classified or un-classified employ of Jefferson Parish, as selected by the parish president. For Jefferson Parish Hospital Service District No. 2 projects, an officer of East Jefferson General Hospital, as designated by the East Jefferson General Hospital Board, shall replace the registered professional engineer selected by the parish president on the committee;
(3)
One (1) representative appointed by the American Institute of Architects (AIA), New Orleans Chapter;
(4)
One (1) representative appointed by the Consulting Engineers Council of Louisiana (CEC-L), New Orleans Area Chapter;
(5)
One (1) representative appointed by the Professional Engineers in Private Practice (PEPP) division of the Louisiana Engineering Society (LES), New Orleans Chapter; and
(6)
In addition to the foregoing and including routine engineering tasks as defined by section 2-926, et seq., at least two (2) of the following: (i) the director of public works; (ii) the director of the engineering department and/or (iii) the director of the capital projects for the Parish of Jefferson. For routine engineering tasks as defined a quorum shall be established by a minimum of five (5) of the foregoing members.
(Ord. No. 18993, § 1, 2-23-94; Ord. No. 19171, § 1, 9-7-94; Ord. No. 19801, § 1, 8-28-96; Ord. No. 24219, § 1, 2-15-12; Ord. No. 24268, § 1, 5-9-12; Ord. No. 24651, § 1, 1-15-14; Ord. No. 25018 , § 8, 9-23-15)

Sec. 2-614. - Terms.

(a)
Initial terms of the members of the evaluation committee appointed by professional societies shall be staggered as follows: The member appointed by the American Institute of Architects shall serve an initial term of one (1) year, the member appointed by the Louisiana Engineering Society shall serve an initial term of eighteen (18) months and the member appointed by the Consulting Engineers Council of Louisiana shall serve an initial term of two (2) years. Thereafter, these members shall serve for two-year terms. The Louisiana registered professional engineer in the classified or un-classified employ of Jefferson Parish shall serve a two-year term and may not serve two (2) consecutive terms. The director of the department directly involved with the project under consideration, or the engineering director acting as his representative, and for hospital service district projects, the hospital president and CEO, or his designee, shall serve ad hoc for those projects.
(b)
Any vacancies on the committee shall be filled for the remainder of the unexpired term, if applicable, in the manner of appointment specified for the vacant position.
(Ord. No. 18993, § 1, 2-23-94; Ord. No. 19171, § 2, 9-7-94; Ord. No. 19801, § 2, 8-28-96; Ord. No. 25576 , § 1, 5-16-18)

(a)
All members of the committee shall serve without compensation and shall not be employees of the parish except for the parish directors and the technical advisor who serve(s) on the committee.
(b)
Any member of the committee who is not an employee of the parish and any person(s) or firm(s) for which said member is working, are prohibited from doing work for Jefferson Parish as either a prime contractor or subcontractor and from doing work for any person or firm appointed to do work for Jefferson Parish during the duration of the member's term and for six (6) months thereafter.
(c)
Members of the technical evaluation committee shall be subject to all rules and regulations governing ethics for public officials in Louisiana during their tenure on the committee.
(d)
The one (1) representative appointed by the American Institute of Architects (AIA), New Orleans Chapter, the one (1) representative appointed by the Consulting Engineers Council of Louisiana (CEC-L), New Orleans Area Chapter, and the one (1) representative appointed by the Professional Engineers in Private Practice (PEPP) division of the Louisiana Engineering Society (LES), New Orleans Chapter, as defined in section 2-611, shall file annually with the Jefferson Parish Director of Ethics and Compliance a notarized and completed Jefferson Parish financial disclosure statement.
(e)
No person or firm submitting as defined by section 2-926(7) nor any officer, employee or representative of any such person shall contact any member of the evaluation committee about a project under consideration by said committee.
(Ord. No. 18993, § 1, 2-23-94; Ord. No. 20883, § 1, 2-9-00; Ord. No. 21593, § 12, 6-5-02; Ord. No. 24269, § 1, 5-9-12)
