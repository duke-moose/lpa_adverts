The seven (7) members of the board shall be residents of Jefferson Parish and be appointed as follows. Each member of the governing authority of Jefferson Parish shall appoint one (1) member. One (1) member of the board shall be a Louisiana Registered Architect; one (1) member shall be a Louisiana Registered Civil Engineer; one (1) member shall be a Louisiana Registered Electrical Engineer; one (1) member shall be a Louisiana Registered Mechanical Engineer; one (1) member shall be a licensed general contractor; and two (2) other members shall be selected at-large from the parish by the governing authority.
