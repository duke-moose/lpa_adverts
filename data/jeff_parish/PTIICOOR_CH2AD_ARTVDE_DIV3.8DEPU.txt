Sec. 2-163.1 - Qualifications, appointment and salary of director.

(a)
Qualifications. The purchasing director shall possess a thorough knowledge of governmental purchasing, demonstrate an ability to manage administrative operations, be qualified by education, training and at least five (5) years of related administrative and/or management experience.
(b)
Appointment. The director of purchasing shall be appointed by the parish president with the approval of the council.
(c)
Salary. The salary of the director of purchasing shall be established by the parish president within the pay grade for the position as defined in the executive pay plan.
(Ord. No. 21171, § 1, 1-10-01; Ord. No. 25736 , § 7, 2-6-19)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 25736 , § 7, adopted Feb. 6, 2019, amended § 2-163.1 and in so doing changed the title from "Qualifications and appointment of director" to "Qualifications, appointment and salary of director," as set out herein.
Sec. 2-163.2. - Duties of director.

The director of purchasing shall be responsible for:
(1)
The development and administration of a system of purchasing for all public works projects, labor, materials, supplies, equipment and services for all departments in the parish which includes authority to issue purchase orders as necessary, within the purchasing requirements promulgated by the Jefferson Parish Council and the Louisiana Legislature;
(2)
The hiring, training and professional development of purchasing personnel.
(Ord. No. 21171, § 1, 1-10-01; Ord. No. 23103, § 3, 7-18-07)
