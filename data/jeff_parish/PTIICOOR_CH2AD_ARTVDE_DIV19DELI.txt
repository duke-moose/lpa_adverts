The department of library and the position of director of library are hereby created, which department and position shall have direct administrative supervision over the parish public library and its branches.
(Code 1961, § 2-500)
Sec. 2-392. - Qualifications, appointment and salary of director.

(a)
Qualifications. The director of library shall be qualified by education, training and prior administrative and/or management experience and shall devote his entire efforts to this purpose for which this department has been created.
(b)
Appointment. The director of library shall be appointed by the parish president with the approval of the council.
(c)
Salary. The salary of the director of library shall be established by the parish president within the pay grade for the position as defined in the executive pay plan.
(Code 1961, § 2-502; Ord. No. 25736 , § 27, 2-6-19)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 25736 , § 27, adopted Feb. 6, 2019, amended § 2-392 and in so doing changed the title from "Qualifications and appointment of director" to "Qualifications, appointment and salary of director," as set out herein.
Sec. 2-393. - Duties of director.

The director of library shall:
(1)
Manage department operations in the parish public library and its branches;
(2)
Provide policy guidance to the parish president and the chief administrative assistant in the area of the parish library and its branches;
(3)
Direct administrative and field staff assigned to this department;
(4)
Formulate and evaluate operating policies, programs and procedures relating to library programs with the advice and consent of the chief administrative assistant;
(5)
Prepare reports on departmental operations evaluating performances against established objectives, and special reports on operating problems or plans as required for review by the chief administrative assistant;
(6)
Coordinate budget formulation and management activities in the department of library;
(7)
Other such activities and/or responsibilities as the parish president may direct.
(Code 1961, § 2-501; Ord. No. 17439, § I, 2-10-88)
