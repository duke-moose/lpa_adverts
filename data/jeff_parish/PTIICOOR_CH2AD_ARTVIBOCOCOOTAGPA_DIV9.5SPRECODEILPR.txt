The specialized review committee is hereby established for the purpose of evaluating technical proposals for all design-build projects or contracts under consideration by Jefferson Parish and shall be composed of: the public works director, or his designee, acting as the committee chairman; a representative of the finance department to be appointed by the finance director; the director of the parish department directly involved in the design-build project; and any other Jefferson Parish employee deemed necessary by the committee chairman. At least one (1) member of the specialized review committee shall be a professional engineer.
(Ord. No. 22716, § 1, 3-22-06)
