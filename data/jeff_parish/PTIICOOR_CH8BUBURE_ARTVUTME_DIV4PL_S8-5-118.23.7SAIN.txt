Where required:
1.
Wherever a floor drain discharges through an oil interceptor, it must first discharge through a sand interceptor when required by the chief plumbing inspector or his designee.
2.
Whenever the discharge of a floor drain may contain solids or semisolids that would be harmful to a drainage system, or tend to choke the system, the discharge must be through a sand interceptor.
