The board shall be composed of sixteen (16) voting members: two (2) members appointed by each councilmember and by the parish president, with one (1) representing commercial seafood interests (wholesalers, retailers, processors or commercial fisherman) and one (1) representing sports fishing interests (recreational fisherman). The council may appoint an alternate member to represent any regular member of the board on their behalf when they are unable to attend a board meeting. Said alternate member shall be authorized to attend board meetings as a voting member of the board when the regular member is absent. The board shall elect a chairman and vice chairman from among its members for a term of one (1) year, beginning with the first scheduled board meeting in the month of January. The chairman shall conduct board meetings in accordance with Robert's Rules of Order. The terms of the board members shall be concurrent with the terms of the appointing councilmembers and the parish president. All board members shall serve at the pleasure of the appointing authority and without compensation.
(Ord. No. 17396, § 2, 12-2-87; Ord. No. 17541, § 1, 7-13-88; Ord. No. 18438, § 1, 1-8-92; Ord. No. 18489, § 1, 4-1-92; Ord. No. 22149, § 1, 3-17-04)

Sec. 2-875.17. - Duties, functions; officers; meetings.

(a)
The board shall have the following duties and functions:
(1)
Coordinate the activities of the parish administration and the council relative to the seafood industry; and
(2)
Recommend policies affecting marine fisheries regarding proposed legislation and the fishermen of the parish, methods of utilizing new species of seafood, increasing seafood production, marketing and processing.
(b)
The board shall select a chairman and vice-chairman and conduct its meetings according to rules and regulations adopted by the board.
(c)
The board shall, at a minimum, meet quarterly.
(Ord. No. 17396, § 4, 12-2-87)

The board shall cooperate with and recommend to the parish economic development council projects which will be beneficial to the fishing industry. The board shall submit the names of three (3) nominees to the parish economic development council. The nominees need not be members of the board.
(Ord. No. 17396, § 5, 12-2-87)

In addition to the members of the board appointed above, the following shall serve as ex-officio members of the board and shall act as technical advisors to the board: a representative of the Louisiana Department of Wildlife and Fisheries, a representative of the Jefferson Parish Department of Environmental Affairs, a representative of the Louisiana Cooperative Extension Service, a representative of the Jefferson Parish Legal Department, the Jefferson Parish Coastal Zone Management Administrator, and the Port Program Manager of the Jefferson Parish Economic Development and Port District.
(Ord. No. 17396, § 6, 12-2-87; Ord. No. 20129, § 1, 8-27-97; Ord. No. 20921, § 3, 3-15-00)

The board may receive and accept unconditional gifts, donations and contributions from individuals and corporations, but no gifts or donations conditionally made shall be accepted without the approval of the parish council. All moneys, property and other things of value given or contributed to the board shall be given to the finance department of the parish and shall be expended or invested in accordance with budgetary regulations of the parish for purposes provided for in section 2-875.17.
