The data processing advisory committee is hereby created to consist of (1) an appointee from the parish president's office; (2) the data processing coordinator; (3) an appointee from the office of research and budget analysis; (4) the director of the department of finance; (5) the director of the department of public works; (6) the director of the personnel department; and (7) an appointee of the parish attorney.
(Ord. No. 19171, § 4, 9-7-94; Ord. No. 19689, § 1, 4-24-96; Ord. No. 25897 , § 4, 1-6-19)

The data processing advisory committee shall be responsible all data processing hardware and software requests of parish departments and agencies; informing the parish administration and council of the advisability and benefits of each request; establishing priorities for implementation of data processing systems; and for evaluating submittals from persons or firms wishing to perform professional services for data processing projects in accordance with the provisions of Division 3.1 of Chapter 2, Article VII of this Code.
(Ord. No. 19171, § 4, 9-7-94; Ord. No. 25897 , § 4, 1-6-19)
