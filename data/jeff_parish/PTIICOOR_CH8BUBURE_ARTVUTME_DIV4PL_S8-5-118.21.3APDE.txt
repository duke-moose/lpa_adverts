Before any device for the prevention of backflow or backsiphonage is installed, it shall have first been certified by a recognized testing laboratory acceptable to the chief plumbing inspector or his designee. Devices installed in a building potable water supply distribution maintained in good working condition by the person or persons responsible for the maintenance of the system.
The chief plumbing inspector or his designee shall inspect routinely such devices and if found to be defective or inoperative shall require the replacement thereof.
