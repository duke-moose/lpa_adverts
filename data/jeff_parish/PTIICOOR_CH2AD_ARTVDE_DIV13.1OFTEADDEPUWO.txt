There is hereby created the office of technical advisor to the department of public works and the position of technical advisor to the department of public works within the department of public works to serve the parish by providing technical advice and support to the administration, council, public works director and all public works groups.
( Ord. No. 25928 , § 1, 12-18-19)

(a)
Qualifications. The technical advisor to the department of public works shall possess the following qualifications
(1)
A four-year degree in engineering with five (5) years' relevant experience and active registration as a professional engineer in the state;
(2)
Five (5) years of experience in local government operations and consulting work: and
(3)
Five (5) years of significant management experience with a public works agency.
(b)
Appointment. The technical advisor to the department of public works shall be appointed by the parish president with the approval of the council.
(c)
Salary. The salary of the technical advisor to the department of public works shall be established by the parish president within the pay grade for the position as defined in the executive pay plan.
( Ord. No. 25928 , § 1, 12-18-19)

The technical advisor to the department of public works' duties shall include but are not limited to the following:
(1)
Assist the public works director with all activities related to public works functions including administration, financial operation, construction, maintenance and policy development in the public works groups;
(2)
Advise parish president and council members on technical related issues;
(3)
Attend and participate in council and council agenda meetings;
(4)
Serve as a replacement to the public works director during his/her absence;
(5)
Prepare and deliver reports and information to parish officials and administration;
(6)
Assist with the handling of complaints received by the administration and council;
(7)
Assist with the interviewing and hiring of public works personnel;
(8)
Assist public works director with the planning of capital improvement projects and capital facilities planning;
(9)
Assist the parish attorney's office with technical related issues pertaining to litigation; and
(10)
Serve as an advisor on the technical evaluation committee.
( Ord. No. 25928 , § 1, 12-18-19)
