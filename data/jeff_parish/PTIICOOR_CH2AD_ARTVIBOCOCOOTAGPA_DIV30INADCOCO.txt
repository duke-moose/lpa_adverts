The insurance advisory and coordinating committee shall be composed of:
(1)
The chief operating officer;
(2)
The director of the department of human resources management;
(3)
The director of the finance department;
(4)
The director of the department of risk management;
(5)
The chief of staff to the parish council;
(6)
The research director or legal analysis director of the research and budget analysis staff of the parish council, or their designee; and
(7)
The parish attorney or his designee, who shall serve as the only nonvoting member.
(Ord. No. 16033, § 2, 5-9-84; Ord. No. 18458, § 1, 2-5-92; Ord. No. 19706, § 1, 5-22-96; Ord. No. 24653, § 1, 1-15-14)

The insurance advisory and coordinating committee shall have the following duties and functions:
(1)
To coordinate the activities of the administration, and the insurance agent for the parish involving the insurance program for Jefferson Parish;
(2)
To recommend to the council any actions necessary for the efficient and effective administration of an insurance program for the Parish of Jefferson;
(3)
To recommend priorities regarding the purchase of any insurance by the Parish of Jefferson;
(4)
To consult with the parish insurance agent, other insurance agents and other interested parties and make a recommendation to the council whenever the need arises regarding the purchase of any insurance by the Parish of Jefferson;
(5)
To review and advise the parish council and the administration regarding any facet of the insurance program.
(Ord. No. 16033, § 3, 5-9-84; Ord. No. 19706, § 2, 5-22-96)

In accordance with the provisions of R.S. 42:1101 et seq., the insurance agent for the Parish of Jefferson shall make no recommendation to the council regarding the purchase of insurance policies nor shall the insurance agent "participate" in any "action" regarding the insurance program of Jefferson Parish, as such terms are defined in R.S. 42:1102; all such recommendations to the council shall be made by and through the insurance advisory and coordinating committee.
(Ord. No. 16033, § 4, 5-9-84)
