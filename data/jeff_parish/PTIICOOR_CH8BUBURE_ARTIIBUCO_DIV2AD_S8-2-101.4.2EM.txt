The code official shall appoint employees subject to the existing civil service laws of the Parish of Jefferson, State of Louisiana. The person or persons appointed in the various technical sections as code enforcement officers (exclusive of secretarial and clerical personnel) shall be persons fully qualified by technical training and experience, in both theory and practice. Such employees shall also be certified in their technical field and registered as required, in the time allotted, by the Louisiana State Uniform Construction Code Council in the field where their enforcement services will be utilized.
