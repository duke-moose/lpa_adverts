Sec. 8-2-104.10.2. - Inspection of manufacturers and fabricators.

When deemed necessary, the code official shall make, or cause to be made, an inspection of materials or assemblies at the point of manufacture or fabrication if such is out of this jurisdiction. The cost of the code official's required inspection(s) shall be borne by the party requesting to use such materials or assemblies in this jurisdiction. A record shall be made of every such examination and inspection and of any apparent violations of the technical codes. The code official may accept certification from third party testing/inspection agencies, the credentials of which are approved by the code official.
